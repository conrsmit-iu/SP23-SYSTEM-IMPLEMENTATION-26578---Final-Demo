Welcome to Pick It Ticket!

Pick It Ticket? Who's that?

Your one-stop shop for all your ticket needs

Have you ever been to a theater and been discomforted by the lack of suitable seating for you or your party? A party of six that couldn't find six seats next to each other? A disabled individual who needed a seat on the edge but simply couldn't find it? Maybe you really wanted a front row seat for this event? Well you won't have to face such issues anymore! We here at Pick It Ticket work with your theater to provide accomodation for any and all seating needs!

How does Pick It Ticket work?

A simple solution for a not so simple problem

When a theater is working with Pick It Ticket, we create a map of any room in the theater and assign each seat a number. When you or your friends buy a ticket, instead of the usual free-for-all that occurs in the theater, you instead are buying the specific seat you chose. When someone's reserved a given seat already, we let both you and the theater know so you can instead look for another spot that works for you! We also offer options to help you find the best arrangements for your needs!
